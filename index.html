About EP TECH Innovation

EP TECH Innovation started as a simple idea — that no one should have to figure out tech alone. What began as a small WhatsApp group has grown into a community of 175+ members sharing scholarships, learning resources, and honest guidance for anyone on the path to becoming a developer, engineer, or tech professional.

CS

I'm Chukwuebuka Samuel (Ebuka) — founder and community manager. I'm a Computer Science student building toward full-stack development and AI, and I started this community because I wanted the kind of support system I wish I'd had when I started.
